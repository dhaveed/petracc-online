== Installation ==
= ONLINE INSTALLATION =
1. Search for "Arena.IM" in wordpress.org and install the free plugin.
2. Activate the plugin.
3. Create your account on [dashboard.arena.im](https://dashboard.arena.im)
4. Go to Settings > Arena and Log In with your Arena.IM account
5. Manage your events on [dashboard.arena.im](https://dashboard.arena.im) and use the Arena button on the Post page to list and embed them to your blog post.

= DOWNLOAD INSTALLATION =
1. Download the plugin via http://go.arena.im/plugin/wp/arena_liveblog.zip and unzip on your plugins directory /wp-content/plugins/.
2. Activate the plugin.
3. Create your account on [dashboard.arena.im](https://dashboard.arena.im)
4. Go to Settings > Arena and Log In with your Arena.IM account
5. Manage your events on [dashboard.arena.im](https://dashboard.arena.im) and use the Arena button on the Post page to list and embed them to your blog post.
Note: You will need a free Arena.IM account: [Create one for free here!](https://dashboard.arena.im)
